# Mackinac Straits Watersports & Rental Co. knowledge base

Source: https://www.mackinacstraitsrentalco.biz/

## About
Mackinac Straits Watersports & Rental Co. is a mobile rental operation based in St. Ignace, Michigan, right on the Straits of Mackinac. They rent jet skis, kayaks, bicycles, electric bikes, and their custom Pon Tiki pontoon boat, and run Mackinac Bridge viewing tours and sunset fireworks tours. All bookings are made by phone, not online.

## Kayak Rentals
- $25 for 1 hour, $35 for 2 hours, $45 for 3 hours, $75 for a full day.
- Discounts for multiple days or multiple kayaks. A delivery fee may be required. A two kayak minimum may apply during peak season.
- Ask about picnic packages.

## Fireworks & Mackinac Bridge Tours
- Fireworks and Sunset Tour: $50 per person, Saturdays from the last weekend of June through Labor Day, 8:30pm to 10:30pm.
- Mackinac Bridge Viewing Tour: $65 per kayak, 8am and 10am tours, must be booked a day in advance or before 9am.

## Pon Tiki Pontoon Cruises
- Fireworks/Sunset: $250 for 4 people.
- Sunset Nights: $175 for 4 people, 2 hours, 8pm to 10pm.
- Sunrise: appointment only, around 5am, price on request.
- Daytime: $100/hour, 11am to 5pm daily, 4 passengers, +$25 per added passenger.
- Add-ons: kayaks, jet skis, lunch, delivery, ice and coolers, daytime activities for a family beach day, mixology class (BYOB), painting class (BYOB).

## Jet Ski Rentals
- Sea Doo GTI (3 to 5 available, 4 stroke, 2 seater, 50 mph): $75 for 30 minutes, $125 for 1 hour, $235 for 2 hours (Mackinac Bridge minimum), $335 for 3 hours, $400 for 4 hours.
- Yamaha VX Waverunner (1 available, 2 seater, fast, reverse, Bluetooth, includes gas and life jackets, 60 mph): $150 for 1 hour, $290 for 2 hours, $420 for 3 hours.
- A valid driver's license and boater safety certificate may be required. Credit card held until return.

- Bookings are by phone only, (517) 528-9545. This is a mobile operation and may not be on site at all times.
