# Mackinac Straits Watersports & Rental Co. knowledge base

Source: https://www.mackinacstraitsrentalco.biz/

## About
Mackinac Straits Watersports & Rental Co. (also known as Mackinac Straits Kayaking) is a
family-run, mobile rental operation based in St. Ignace, Michigan, right on the Straits of
Mackinac. We rent jet skis, kayaks, bicycles, electric bikes, and our custom-built Pon Tiki
pontoon, and run Mackinac Bridge viewing tours and sunset/fireworks cruises. The main
contact is Mike.

## How to book
We are a mobile operation and may not be on site at all times, so all rentals are booked by
phone, not online. Call (517) 528-9545. Reservations for jet skis or kayaks must be
confirmed the day of. Phone calls are accepted daily starting at 8am.

## Fireworks & Mackinac Bridge tours
- Fireworks and Sunset Tour: Saturdays, last weekend of June through Labor Day, 8:30pm to
  10:30pm, $50 per person.
- Mackinac Bridge Viewing Tour: 8am and 10am tours (ask about later times), must be booked
  a day in advance or before 9am, $65 per kayak.

No online booking. Call (517) 528-9545 to reserve, since we're a mobile operation.

**What's the cheapest way to try a kayak?**

Yes, the Fireworks and Sunset Tour runs Saturdays (last weekend of June through Labor

A valid driver's license and boater safety certificate may be required depending on age
