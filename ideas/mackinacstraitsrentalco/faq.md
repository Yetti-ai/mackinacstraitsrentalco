# Mackinac Straits Watersports & Rental Co. FAQ

## How do I book a rental?
Call (517) 528-9545. This is a mobile operation, so bookings are taken by phone, not online. Phone calls are accepted starting at 8am daily.

## How much do kayak rentals cost?
$25 for 1 hour, $35 for 2 hours, $45 for 3 hours, and $75 for a full day. Discounts are available for multiple days or multiple kayaks, and a delivery fee may apply.

## What does the Mackinac Bridge kayak tour cost?
$65 per kayak. Tours run at 8am and 10am, and must be booked a day in advance or before 9am the same day.

## What does the fireworks and sunset kayak tour cost?
$50 per person, on Saturdays from the last weekend of June through Labor Day, 8:30pm to 10:30pm.

## What are the Pon Tiki pontoon rental rates?
Fireworks/Sunset package is $250 for 4 people, Sunset Nights is $175 for 4 people (2 hours, 8pm-10pm), Daytime is $100/hour from 11am-5pm for 4 passengers (plus $25 per added person), and Sunrise is by appointment only around 5am (price on request).

## What does jet ski rental cost?
Sea Doo GTI: $75 for 30 minutes, $125 for 1 hour, $235 for 2 hours (Mackinac Bridge minimum), $335 for 3 hours, $400 for 4 hours. Yamaha VX Waverunner: $150 for 1 hour, $290 for 2 hours, $420 for 3 hours.

## Do I need a license to rent a jet ski?
A valid driver's license and boater safety certificate may be required. A credit card is held until the jet ski is returned.

## Where are you located?
499 N State St., St Ignace, Michigan 49781, right on the Straits of Mackinac.

## Are you affiliated with the Indian Village gift shop?
No, Mackinac Straits Watersports & Rental Co. is not affiliated with the Indian Village gift shop.

## What else can I rent?
Jet skis, kayaks, bicycles, electric bikes, and the custom Pon Tiki pontoon boat.
